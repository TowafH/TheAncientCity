What loot can be found in an Ancient City?

In Minecraft's Ancient City, you'll find valuable loot in chests, including enchanted books, echo shards, disc fragments, diamonds, and potions. Rare items like enchanted golden apples and name tags are also available.

Unique Items

  • Enchanted Books: With rare enchantments like Swift Sneak.
  • Echo Shards: Used to craft the Recovery Compass.
  • Disc Fragments: Nine fragments can be combined to create the music disc "5.
  • Diamonds and Diamond Gear: Sometimes enchanted with powerful enchantments.
  • Potions: Typically regeneration or healing potions.
  • Other Rare Items: Such as name tags, enchanted golden apples, and amethyst shards.
