Do mobs spawn in the Ancient City?

Despite being in the deep dark, Mob's don't actually spawn in the Ancient City. The warden probably scared them off!

Mobs that are normally found in the dark like Zombies, Spiders, Skeletons, and Creepers won't spawn in the Ancient City!

The only mob you should be worried about is summoning the Warden!



Exploring the Ancient City

While you may have the strongest armor in the game, it won't do you well up against the warden!

To avoid the Warden, here are some strategies!

    1. Avoid Detection

  1. Crouching: The Warden is blind but can hear any movement. Crouch to reduce noise and avoid activating Sculk Shriekers.
  2. Projectiles: Distract the Warden by throwing items like snowballs or eggs away from your location. This can lure it away.

    3. 2. Have an Escape Plan

  3. Sneak Away: When the Warden is distracted, crouch and sneak away from the area.

    5. 3. Worst-case Scenario

  5. Ender Pearl: Using an Ender pearl to teleport far from where the Warden can spawn can save your life and items!
  6. Totem/Potions: A totem of undying can prevent you from dying instantly from the Warden and plan for an escape route!
