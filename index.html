What is Minecraft's Ancient City?

The Ancient city is an eerie structure found deep underground in Minecraft. The city is filled with valuable loot, unique structures, and is home to the Warden.

Exploring the Ancient City requires extreme caution and patience. The Warden has keen senses and picks up on any type of vibration. This guide will explore how you can avoid an encounter with the Warden.

The city is rich in history and lore, with intricate designs and hidden secrets waiting to be uncovered by brave adventurers.
